About Us

An online purchase should only require credit card information, address, and phone number. If the website is also asking for your social security number, you should be suspicious. This information can be used to steal your identity and should be unnecessary for an online purchase. Online shopping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the Internet using a web browser or a mobile app. Global e-commerce sales are expected to hit $5.5 trillion in 2022. Around 76% of U.S. adults shop online. Over half of consumers prefer shopping in a physical store. Smartphones are slowly becoming the preferred way to order online.18-Mar-2022
